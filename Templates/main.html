Disaster tweet classification using Random Forest

Tweet

  • Any tweet of upto 280 characters which may or may not contain information about a disaster.
  • Leading and trailing whitespace will be trimmed.
  • Required

Keyword

  • Any word or phrase within the tweet or related to it that is thought to contain information about a disaster.
  • Leading and trailing whitespace will be trimmed.
  • Optional

Examples

    • Tweet:

      George Njenga the hero saved his burning friend from a razing wildfire http://t.co/Ywkk26arAG

    • Keyword:

      razed


    • Tweet:

      I was on my way to Gary but all the Chicago entrances was closed due to a bridge collapsed ?????? I hope they let us through tomorrow

    • Keyword:

      collapsed
